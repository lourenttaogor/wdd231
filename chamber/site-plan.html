Real Estate - Site Plan

Site Name: D'Laurels Estate

Realex is a real estate website that allows property owners amd property agents list their properties for rent, lease or sale. It'll help house hunters finds available properties for lease or sale.

Site Purpose

  • To help house hunters find available properties for rent or sale
  • To help property owners and house agents list their properties online

Scenarios

  • How can I find my desire house?
  • How do i contact property agent or owner?
  • How do i find properties for rent or sale in a particular location from the comfort of my home
